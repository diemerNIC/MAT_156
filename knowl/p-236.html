Absorption Laws.

Logic
\begin{align*} p\myand(p\myor q)\amp\Leftrightarrow p\\ p\myand(\notp\,\myor\,q)\amp\Leftrightarrow\,p\myand q\\ p\myor(p\myand q)\amp\Leftrightarrow\,p\\ p\myor(\notp\,\myand\,q)\amp\Leftrightarrow\,p\myor q \end{align*}
Boolean
\begin{align*} A(A+B)\amp=A\\ A(\nota+B)\amp=AB\\ A+AB\amp=A\\ A+\nota{}B\amp=A+B \end{align*}
