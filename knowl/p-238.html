Here are all the laws of logic discussed so far:

Law Logic Boolean Algebra
Identity \(p \myand{} 1 \myleq{} p\) \(A \cdot 1 = A\)
\(p \myor{}1 \myleq{} 1\) \(A+1 = 1\)
\(p \myand{} 0 \myleq{} 0\) \(A \cdot 0 = 0\)
\(p \myor{}0 \myleq{} p\) \(A+0 = A\)
Idempotent \(p \myand{} p \myleq{} p\) \(AA = A\)
\(p \myor{} p \myleq{} p\) \(A+A = A\)
Complement \(\mylnot{(\mylnot{p})} \myleq{} p\) \(\mybnot{\nota{}} = A\)
\(p \myand{} \mylnot{p} \myleq{} 0\) \(A\nota{} = 0\)
\(p \myor{}\mylnot{p} \myleq{} 1\) \(A+ \nota{} = 1\)
Commutative \(p \myand{} q \myleq{} q \myand{} p\) \(AB = BA\)
\(p \myor{}q \myleq{} q \myor{} p\) \(A+ B = B + A\)
Associative \((p \myand{} q) \myand{} r \myleq{} p \myand{} (q \myand{} r) \) \((AB)C = A(BC) \)
\((p \myor{} q) \myor{} r \myleq{} p \myor{} (q \myor{} r) \) \((A+B)+C = A+(B+C) \)
De Morgan's \(\mylnot{(p \myand{} q)} \myleq{} \notp{} \myor{} \notq{}\) \(\mybnot{AB} = \nota{} + \notb{}\)
\(\mylnot{(p \myor{} q)} \myleq{} \notp{} \myand{} \notq{}\) \(\mybnot{A+B} = \nota{} ~ \notb{}\)
Distributive \(p\myand{}(q\myor{}r) \myleq{} (p\myand{}q)\myor{}(p\myand{}r)\) \(A(B+C) = AB + AC\)
\(p\myor{}(q\myand{}r) \myleq{} (p\myor{}q)\myand{}(p\myor{}r)\) \(A+BC = (A+B)(A+C)\)
Absorption \(p\myand{}(p\myor{}q) \myleq{} p\) \(A(A+B) = A\)
\(p\myand{}(\notp{}\myor{}q) \myleq{} p\myand{}q\) \(A(\nota{} + B) = AB\)
\(p\myor{}(p\myand{}q) \myleq{} p\) \(A + AB = A\)
\(p\myor{}(\notp{}\myand{}q) \myleq{} p\myor{}q\) \(A+ \nota{}B = A+ B \)
