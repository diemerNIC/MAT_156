Answer 2.1.6.1.
"Jason does his marking or goes to a movie but not both" is true provided exactly one of the two conditions is true. If he does his marking, then he cannot also go to a movie or else the exclusive disjunction is false. If he goes to a movie, then he cannot also do his marking or the statement is false. Truth of the statement means he doesn't do both, and he doesn't do neither.
