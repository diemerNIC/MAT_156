The first term is already given, \(a_0=3\text{.}\) Then
\begin{align*} {a_1} \amp = {\left( {3 - 1} \right)^2} + 10 = {2^2} + 10 = 14\\ {a_2} \amp = {\left( {14 - 1} \right)^2} + 10 = {13^2} + 10 = 179\\ {a_3} \amp = {\left( {179 - 1} \right)^2} + 10 = {178^2} + 10 = 31694 \end{align*}
So the first four terms are 3, 14, 179, 31694.
