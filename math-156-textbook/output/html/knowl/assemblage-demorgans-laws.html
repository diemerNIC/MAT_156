De Morgan's Laws.

Logic

\begin{align*} \sim\!(p{\wedge} q)\amp\Leftrightarrow\,\sim\!{p}\,{\vee}\sim\!{q}\\ \sim\!(p{\vee} q)\amp\Leftrightarrow\,\sim\!{p}\,{\wedge}\sim\!{q}. \end{align*}

Boolean

\begin{align*} \overline{A\cdot B}\amp =\overline{A}+\overline{B}\\ \overline{A+B}\amp =\overline{A}\cdot\overline{B} \end{align*}
