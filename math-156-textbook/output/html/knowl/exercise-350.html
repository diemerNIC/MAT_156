\(\ \)The following conditional statement is true: If Jason is eaten by nears, he will not finish his marking. Given that, answer the following questions.
  1. Jason is eaten by bears. Did he finish his marking?
  2. Jason is not eaten by bears. Did he finish his marking?
  3. Jason finished his marking. Was he eaten by bears?
  4. Jason did not finish his marking. Was he eaten by bears?
