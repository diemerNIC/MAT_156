\(\ \)Consider the conditional If Jason is late and has not called his wife, she will be worried. Which of the following is the corresponding contrapositive statement \((\contrapositive)\text{?}\)
  1. If Jason’s wife is not worried, then he is not late and did call her.
  2. If Jason’s wife is not worried, then he is not late or did call her.
  3. If Jason’s wife is worried, then he is late and has not called her.
  4. If Jason’s wife is not worried, then he is late and did not call her.
