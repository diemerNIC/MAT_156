\(\ \)The following conditional statement is true: If ettercaps are green, then toves are slithy. Given that, answer the following questions.

  1. Toves are slithy. Are ettercaps green?

  2. The hare reads the Times Colonist. Will the tortoise take out the recycling?

  3. The tortoise takes out the recycling. Does the hare read the Times Colonist?
