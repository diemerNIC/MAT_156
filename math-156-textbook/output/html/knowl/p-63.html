This sort of calculation is very helpful when doing unit conversions. 1  For example, if we know that a certain length of time is 54 hours long and we would prefer to give it in terms of days and hours, then

\begin{align*} 54 \text{ hours} \amp =\underbrace{\text{days}}_{\text{quotient}}\times 24+\underbrace{\text{hours}}_{\text{remainder}}\\ \amp = 2\times 24+6 \end{align*}

so 54 hours \(=\) 2 days plus 6 hours.
