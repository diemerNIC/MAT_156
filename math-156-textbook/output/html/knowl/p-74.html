You'll notice that we have a bit of a problem: if we put the fractional/decimal part 0.4 on the next line, then we'll have a repeat of the second line, and then the next three lines will also repeat, and so on. What this means is that \(0.3_{10}\) is a repeating decimal in octal: \(0.3_{10}=0.2\overline{3146}_8\text{.}\)
