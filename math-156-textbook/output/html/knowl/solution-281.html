Solution 2.5.8.4.1.

The four identity laws follow from the following observations: The \(p\myand 0\) column is the same as \(0\text{,}\) the \(p\myor 0\) and the \(p\myand 1\) columns are the same as \(p\text{,}\) and the \(p\myor 1\) column is the same as \(1\text{.}\)
