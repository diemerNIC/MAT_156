By comparing the results of 1-15, we see that the following expressions are logically equivalent to \(\biconditional\text{:}\)
  1. \(\displaystyle \sim\!{p}{\leftrightarrow}\sim\!{q}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle (\conditional){\wedge}(\converse)\)
  3. \(\displaystyle (\conditional){\wedge}(\inverse)\)
(You may or may not have noticed that it is also equivalent to \(\sim\!{p}{\oplus} q\text{.}\))
