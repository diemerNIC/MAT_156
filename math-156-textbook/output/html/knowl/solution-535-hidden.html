3 years is 36 months, so we have a 36-term sequence starting with \(1, 2, 4, 8, \ldots\) The \(\nth{}\) term will be \({a_n} = 1{\left( 2 \right)^{n - 1}}\text{,}\) so the \(\upth{36}\) term will be \({a_{36}} = 1{\left( 2 \right)^{35}} = 34,359,738,368\text{,}\) which is a tad larger than the total population of Transylvania.
