Theorem 2.2.16. De Morgan's Laws.

Let \(p\) and \(q\) be any propositions. Then

\begin{gather*} \sim\!(p{\wedge} q)\Leftrightarrow\,\sim\!{p}\,{\vee}\sim\!{q}\\ \sim\!(p{\vee} q)\Leftrightarrow\,\sim\!{p}\,{\wedge}\sim\!{q} \end{gather*}

where the symbol \(\Leftrightarrow\) is to be read as "is logically equivalent to".
